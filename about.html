About our company

We are a privately owned UK company based in the North East of England and supply some of the finest oak and other wooden furniture direct from our numerous factories from around the world. Because we buy in volume by the container load, you can be sure that you are cutting out the middlemen and are obtaining the best quality furniture available for your money today.

Buying Furniture From Us?

We are one of the leading online retailers of high quality solid oak, pine & painted furniture in the UK. We have over 25 years experience in the furniture business and are firm believers in the maxim that how you make people feel is just as important as what you make.

Product Range

We feature a wide range of exclusive designs on our website with many items in stock for immediate delivery. We are continually developing new product so if you can’t see what you’re looking for please give us a call and we’ll be delighted to discuss your requirements. We’re very confident we can help.

Location

Some of our customers choose to visit our warehouse near the City of Durham which is within easy reach of Newcastle, Sunderland, York, Leeds, Manchester, Edinburgh and Glasgow and beyond. If you’d like to see the quality of the furniture prior to purchase, simply give us a call so that we can ensure we have the items you are interested in.

Opening Times

Our Website is open 24 hours a day 7 days a week, and all orders can be ran through our secure card payment facility in the comfort of your own home. Should you wish to pop along and verify the quality of the furniture you intend to buy or ask for any advice on the telephone, our offices, show area and phone lines are open Monday – Friday 9am until 5pm. Bank Holiday opening times will be advertised on our website.

Quality and Prices

We pride ourselves on offering superb quality products at great prices. We continually monitor market prices and we’re confident we can offer you extremely competitive prices and value for money.
